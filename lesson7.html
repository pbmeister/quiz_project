Lesson 7: CSRF (Advanced)

Complex CSRF attacks target multi-step actions or APIs. Anti-CSRF tokens and SameSite cookies help ensure requests are legitimate. Content: Complex CSRF attacks can target multi-step processes, like banking transfers. If an app doesn’t verify each step belongs to the authentic user, attackers can trick them into completing actions automatically. Enforcing SameSite cookies ensures that sensitive requests only come from the user’s own session, strengthening CSRF protection.
Takeaway: Always verify user intent and use secure cookie settings to protect multi-step actions.
